# Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about HelloMnemo.

---

## General

### What is HelloMnemo?
HelloMnemo is a mobile app for capturing, organizing, and recalling important information — notes, images, voice recordings, and web links — all in one place.

### Is HelloMnemo free?
HelloMnemo offers a free tier with core features. Premium plans unlock additional storage, advanced search, and exclusive features. See the app for current pricing.

### Which platforms does HelloMnemo support?
HelloMnemo is available on **iOS** (iPhone and iPad) and **Android** devices.

## Account & Data

### How do I reset my password?
1. On the login screen, tap **Forgot Password?**.
2. Enter your registered email address.
3. Check your inbox for a reset link and follow the instructions.

### Can I use HelloMnemo on multiple devices?
Yes. Sign in with the same account on all your devices. Your memories sync automatically via the cloud.

### How do I export my data?
Go to **Profile** → **Settings** → **Privacy** → **Export My Data**. You'll receive a downloadable archive of all your memories.

### What happens to my data if I delete my account?
All data associated with your account is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion, in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

---

## Memories

### Can I recover a deleted memory?
Deleted memories are moved to the **Trash** folder and kept for 30 days. To restore one:
1. Go to **Profile** → **Trash**.
2. Tap the memory you want to restore.
3. Tap **Restore**.

After 30 days, memories in Trash are permanently deleted.

### Is there a limit on how many memories I can store?
Free accounts have a storage limit. Premium accounts get significantly more storage. Check **Profile** → **Settings** → **Storage** to see your current usage.

### Can I share memories with people who don't have the app?
Yes. You can generate a shareable link for any memory. The recipient can view it in a web browser without needing the app.

## Technical

### Why isn't the app syncing?
- Ensure you have an active internet connection.
- Try logging out and back in.
- Check that background app refresh is enabled in your device settings.
- If the issue persists, contact support.

### How do I report a bug?
Go to **Profile** → **Settings** → **Help & Feedback** → **Report a Bug**, or email us at support@hellomnemo.com.
